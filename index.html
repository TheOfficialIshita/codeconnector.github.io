So what’s this all about?

A free start

A leap forward

A local compass

Are you trying to pick up modern skills in coding or tech, but you're not in a position to quit your job, commit to an intense school schedule, or take on large upfront / deferred education costs?


If you said yes, keep reading.

We're here to help.


Our mission is to set everyday people up with coding or tech assitance, education, and tools for free or the cheapest cost possible. We want to see individuals, no matter their work schedule or finacial situation, connect, grow, and thrive through technology.
