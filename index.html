A local compass

Our goal is to provide everyday people with coding education and tools at little to no cost, so that communities can grow, thrive, and communicate.

And what's being offered?

Workshops

A beginner, project-based class that, over the course of 3 sessions, will help you build out experiments in a various coding languages.

Code Co-ops

These are group practice and study sessions, where you’ll be able to level up with other students and mentors in your community.

Events

This is where we’ll hand it off to a local leaders or out-of-towner to lead a talk or discussion on a specific programming topic.

Resources

For the people that aren’t able to make it out to our workshops, co-ops, and events, these tools should should do just the trick.
